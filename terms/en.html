Penalties

Fines include cancellation fees, delay fees, overnight fees, unpaved road fees, loading and download fees in vital areas.

Delay Penalty

The delayed charges by truck type are as follows:

  • Trailer: If the destination is internal and delays exceed two and a half hours, a fine of SAR 100 and SAR 50 per extra hour shall be imposed up to the overnight wage. If the destination is external and there is a delay of more than three and a half hours, a fine of SAR 200 and SAR 50 per additional hour shall be imposed at the maximum overnight wage.
  • Sixtruck: If the destination is inside and delays exceed two and a half hours, a fine of SAR 100 and SAR 40 per extra hour up to the maximum overnight wage. If the destination is external and there is a delay of more than three and a half hours, a fine of SAR 200 and SAR 50 per additional hour shall be imposed at the maximum overnight wage.
  • Lorry: If the destination is internal and delays exceed two and a half hours, a fine of SAR 75 and SAR 25 is imposed for each additional hour up to the overnight wage. If the destination is external and there is a delay of more than three and a half hours, a fine of SAR 150 and SAR 25 per additional hour shall be imposed at the maximum overnight wage.
  • Dyana: If the destination is internal and delays exceed two and a half hours, a fine of SAR 50 and SAR 20 per additional hour shall be imposed up to the overnight wage. If the destination is external and there is a delay of more than three and a half hours, a fine of SAR 75 and SAR 25 per additional hour shall be imposed at the maximum overnight wage.

Cancellation Fine

The cancellation fees by truck type are as follows:

  • Trailer: Before the provider accepts the order, cancellation is without charge. Upon acceptance, a cancellation fine of SAR 100 applies. After the driver arrives, a fine of SAR 200 applies. If the goods are loaded, SAR 400 is deducted, plus SAR 100 per waiting hour.
  • Sixtruck: Before the provider accepts the order, cancellation is without charge. Upon acceptance, a cancellation fine of SAR 100 applies. After the driver arrives, a fine of SAR 150 applies. If the goods are loaded, SAR 350 is deducted, plus SAR 100 per waiting hour.
  • Lorry: Before the provider accepts the order, cancellation is without charge. After the application is accepted, a fine of SAR 75 applies. After the driver arrives at the loading site, a fine of SAR 120 applies. If the goods are loaded, SAR 250 is deducted, plus SAR 50 per waiting hour.
  • Dyanna: Before the provider accepts the order, cancellation is without charge. Upon acceptance, a fine of SAR 50 applies. After the driver arrives, a fine of SAR 100 applies. If the goods are loaded, SAR 150 is deducted, plus SAR 50 per waiting hour.

Overnight Fine

  • Trailer (non-refrigerated and frozen): SAR 300 for the first day, SAR 900 for each additional day.
  • Trailer (refrigerated and frozen): SAR 400 for the first day, SAR 1800 for each additional day.
  • Sixtruck (non-refrigerated and frozen): SAR 250 for the first day, SAR 800 for each additional day.
  • Sixtruck (refrigerated and frozen): SAR 400 for the first day, SAR 1700 for each additional day.
  • Lorry (non-refrigerated and frozen): SAR 200 for the first day, SAR 650 for each additional day.
  • Lorry (refrigerated and frozen): SAR 300 for the first day, SAR 1350 for each additional day.
  • Dyanna (non-refrigerated and frozen): SAR 150 for the first day, SAR 600 for each additional day.
  • Dyanna (refrigerated and frozen): SAR 250 for the first day, SAR 900 for each additional day.

Fine for Unpaved Roads

Additional charges are levied for the use of unpaved roads based on the distance traveled in kilometers:

  • Trailer: SAR 30 per kilometer.
  • Sixtruck: SAR 30 per kilometer.
  • Lorry: SAR 20 per kilometer.
  • Dyanna: SAR 15 per kilometer.

The customer shall bear any damage caused to the goods or truck as a result of using these roads, including the costs of towing the truck if it is disrupted.

Fine for Loading and Downloading in Vital Areas

Additional charges are levied when loading or downloading in vital areas, such as airports, central cities, and ports. Other areas can be added at the discretion of the Estareh platform:

  • Trailer (non-refrigerated and frozen): SAR 400.
  • Trailer (refrigerated and frozen): SAR 600.
  • Sixtruck: SAR 350.
  • Sixtruck (refrigerated and frozen): SAR 450.
  • Lorry (normal): SAR 200.
  • Lorry (refrigerated and frozen): SAR 250.
  • Dyanna (normal): SAR 100.
  • Dyanna (refrigerated and frozen): SAR 150.
